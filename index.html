Katharine Yuan WDD 130

My name is Katharine Yuan. I was born and raised Provo, Utah. I am a family history research major at Brigham Young University-Idaho. I enjoy knitting, logic puzzles, reading, history, and playing my violin. I studied German in high school and college. I specialize in genealogical research in Northwest Germany. I lived in Washington state for three years while my ex-husband was in the army. Mt. St. Helens was one of my favorite places to visit while I lived in Washington. Mt. Ranier is pretty cool too. I've recently picked up computer and web development as a hobby. An interesting quote about genealogy that I like: "Stories have to be told or they die, and when they die, we can't remember who we are or why we're here." - Sue Monk Kidd

Utah

Utah is located in the Mountain West or "Jello belt." It is the home of many national parks and national monuments. One of the most prominent features-and also an image on the state license plate-is Delicate Arch, which is located in Arches National Park.
